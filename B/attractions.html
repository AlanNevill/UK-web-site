Local attractions and places to visit

Meadow Green farm is a short drive away from the wealth of attractions Pembrokeshire has to offer. There are blue flag beaches, coastal paths, castles, theme parks as well as local arts and crafts.

Each cottage has all the current leaflets and guests are encouraged to leave their tips in the cottage's visitors' book.

For a comprehensive list of what's on and where Visit Pembrokeshire.

Below are some of the attractions recommended by guests.

  • Folly Farm
  • Marros riding centre
  • Dinosaur Park Tenby
  • Skomer Island puffins
  • Bosherton lilly ponds
  • St. Davids, Britain's smallest city
  • Manor Wildlife Park
  • Heatherton activity park
  • Oakwood theme park
  • Mackerel fishing
  • Caldy island
  • Pembroke Castle
  • Colby woodland gardens
  • Carmarthen steam railway
