Meadow Green Farm nestles in 23 acres of rolling green meadows in perfect Pembrokeshire, on the outskirts of the the pretty and bustling seaside village of Saundersfoot.
There are three, newly refurbished, contemporary self-catering cottages to provide you with an enchanting holiday. Each is fully stocked to provide a home away from home and have their own private outdoor space with tables, chairs and gas BBQs.
Everyone is well catered for at Meadow Green Farm: for couples a quiet haven of tranquillity, for families we have swings, a playhouse as well as numerous outdoor and indoor toys, books and board games. For fishermen our two lakes can be fished. Animal lovers may bring their own dogs and you can pet our friendly sheep and collect eggs daily from our free-range chickens. For those who love the outdoors you are free to explore our 23 acres of meadows and woods and take the many footpaths which lead from our land.
Within walking distance of Saundersfoot and a short drive to numerous blue flag beaches and fantastic seaside resorts such as Tenby as well as family attractions including Oakwood Park, Folly Farm, Manor House and Heatherton Park.

We look forward to welcoming you to Meadow Green Farm.
