Meadowsweet Cottage

Meadowsweet has been renovated throughout to a high standard. Beautifully and stylishly furnished with great attention to detail it has a contemporary look whilst still retaining a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

The spacious kitchen/diner is fitted with a dishwasher, washing machine / dryer, microwave, fridge, freezer, hob and oven. There is also a comprehensive range of cook and tableware. Double doors lead from the kitchen to an enclosed courtyard area.

A comfortable lounge with cosy sofas to relax on after a busy day on the beach is complete with flat screen TV, DVD player and a selection of games and books.

The staircase from the lounge leads up to the three bedrooms on the first floor. Both the super king and double bed bedrooms have double aspect views over the meadows. The twin is equipped with two generous single beds.

The bathroom located on the first floor has a bath, wash basin and w.c. A separate walk in power shower room with wc can be found on the ground floor.

Enjoy al fresco dinning on the sunny patio sun trap complete with comfortable garden furniture and gas bar-b-que.

Meadowsweet has gas central heating and towels and bed linen are provided. All electricity, heating and wi-fi are included in the tariff.

Pets are welcome at Meadowsweet however smoking is not permitted inside the property.
