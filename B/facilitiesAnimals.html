Our Animals

Our Ryeland sheep are family pets who like to be petted and hand fed. The ewes are named after flowers and the boys after herbs.

Maisy our border collie is 6 years old and has helped many children to overcome their fear of dogs. She adores having a ball thrown for her.

Our old hen, Hetty, runs free range all over the farm. Our young chicks will be providing eggs for breakfast soon!

The two cats, Snowball and Leo are mother and son. They are a pair of grumpies and prefer to be left alone to bask in the sunshine.

Pigs and ducks should be arriving in May.
