Our facilities

Our farm is a relaxing, welcoming holiday for couples and families whatever your age. Our youngest guest has been eight weeks and our eldest 90.

Our guests enjoy the books, board games, puzzles and toys from the beach hut. Leisurely walks around the 23 acres through woodland trails or around the fishing lakes. Pet and feed our friendly sheep, chickens and Maisy dog.

For guests with more energy there is fishing, trampolining and running or walking the nearby coastal footpaths.

Following a fun filled day at the blue flag beaches or the awarding local attractions such as Folly Farm, a warm welcome awaits you.
