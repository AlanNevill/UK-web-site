Cowslip Cottage

Cowslip cottage has been sympathetically renovated to provide a comfortable holiday retreat with stylish furnishings in keeping with the traditional beams and cottage style windows.

Reached via a private enclosed garden it has an open-plan lounge, kitchen/diner. The lounge is equipped with flat screen TV, DVD player and a selection of books.

The kitchen includes dishwasher, fridge/freezer, microwave and hob and oven. There is also a comprehensive range of cook and tableware.

A pretty double bedroom, with fresh white linen, provides views over the lake and ample hanging and storage space.

A cosy twin bedroom provides two generous single beds and storage space.

The modern bathroom contains a walk in shower, sink, heated towel rail and w.c.

Enjoy al fresco dinning in the secluded sunny garden with comfortable patio furniture and gas bar-b-que.

Cowslip has oil central heating. Towels and bed linen are provided. All electricity, heating and WiFi are included in the tariff.

Pets are welcome at Cowslip however smoking is not permitted inside the property.
